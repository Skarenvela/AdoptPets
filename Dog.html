Before you adopt a dog!

Why should you get a Dog?

- Simple, they’re nice to have company with and full of energy to give to you.

- If you have the time and day and a routine, know that you are able to take it out for a walk or take it to the dog park, then get a dog!

- If you don’t mind doing all the cleaning and enjoy them in your space when you get home or sleeping, then get a doggo.

- If you like that bestfriend in your face type of energy then get a dog

The cost of Dog?

- Well the cost of Dog is normally around $2,000-$6,000. Costs associated with dog care include food, toys, leashes, a crate, training, grooming, spay/neuter surgery, routine vet care and, sometimes, emergency vet care. So make sure you’re financially stable and ready. Dogs need quite a bit of interaction with their humans in order to be happy. If you’re very busy and spend little time at home, a dog might not be a suitable pet for you, unless you can bring your dog along when you’re away from home.


For your safety

- Understand your dog needs, and understanding their reaction base on certain smells or taste.

- Remember to put a leash when going for a walk outside with your dog.

- Understand your Dog/Puppies are coming from a broken home or just lost, so its going to take time to get use to knowing their new owners; Patience is key.

- Make sure to give him/her your love and affection.

- All dogs are cute and sweet including Pit Bulls, it just always depends on how you raised and teach them.
